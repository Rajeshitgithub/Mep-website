About Us

MEP EQUIPMENTS PVT. LTD. is an Engineering Company offering quality engineering EQUIPMENT AND ITS SERVICES to clients. The company has been set up to provide ELECTROMECHANICALLY Equipment's for industrial & laboratory to cater the needs of HVAC, Electrical, for pharma, lab, hospitals and complete solutions for supplying and contracts of Engineering Equipments, Mechanical and electrical Retrofit works. The Company is mainly focused in supplying & executing the Equipments and Services for HVAC, Electrical, Clean Rooms and similar allied projects. We take all kinds of retrofit job works on Turnkey Basis.

MEP Equipments Pvt. Ltd. Provide Solutions to its customer with the best available Product and services at a reasonable cost. It has the vision to become one of best Suppliers & contractors for Electromechanically Engineering Goods & Equipments domain. We ensure that our deliverable are an “exact Fit” to client need by understanding requirements, close and regular contacts, periodic quality checks and visit “on-site” locations. Additionally company deploys the technical personnel of various levels at client office for any specific assignment or based on the client long /short-term assignment as required from time to time.
